## SLIDE 5: POSSESSIVE ADJECTIVES - INTRODUCTION
### How to Show Possession

**Visual:** Table with examples

| Person | Possessive | Example |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| I | MY | MY father |
| YOU | YOUR | YOUR mother |
| HE | HIS | HIS brother |
| SHE | HER | HER sister |
| WE | OUR | OUR family |
| THEY | THEIR | THEIR parents |

**Teacher Explains:**
- "We use these words to show something belongs to someone"
- "MY = meu/minha"
- "YOUR = seu/sua"
- "HIS = dele"
- "HER = dela"

**Examples with Gestures:**
- Point to self: "MY family"
- Point to student: "YOUR family"
- Point to male: "HIS family"
- Point to female: "HER family"
